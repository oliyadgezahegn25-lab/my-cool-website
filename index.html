System Quick Start Guide

1. Add Item & PDF Catalog
Use the left sidebar form to enter inventory item details and attach catalogue PDFs directly or paste spec links.
2. Edit Details Anytime
Click the Edit button on any row to adjust item names, stock quantity, unit prices, or catalog links.
3. Record Sales & Sample Releases
Click Sell to log items sold or issued as samples/credit, specify the destination client, quantity, and date.
4. Sales History Menu
Click Sales History Menu at the top navigation bar to view past sales logs as interactive summary cards. Click any entry to inspect its detailed record.
