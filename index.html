Hello! Welcome to Labaid Trading PLC

Here is a quick guide to navigating your inventory system:

1. Adding Inventory

Use the left-hand form to register new products, quantities, prices, and optional PDF specification sheets.

2. Catalog PDF Management

View attached catalog PDFs directly from the table, remove existing links, or upload/replace PDFs anytime.

3. Processing Transactions

Click "Sell" on any item to log sales, credit units, or samples while instantly auto-updating stock counts.

4. Past Transactions & Filter

Click "Menu & Past Transactions" on the top-right to view complete sales history filtered by Week, Month, or Year.
